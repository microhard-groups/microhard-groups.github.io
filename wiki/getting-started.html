Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Go to the homepage and click Sign Up. Enter your email and choose a strong password.

2. Verify Your Email

Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a verification email from Firebase. Click the link to verify your account.

3. Sign In

Return to the site and sign in with your email and password.

4. Start Chatting

Use the search bar to find friends by email, then start messaging instantly.
