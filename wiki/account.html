Account & Security

Password Reset

Currently, password reset is handled through Firebase. Use the "Forgot Password" option if available, or create a new account.

Email Verification

You must verify your email before you can sign in and use the chat.

Account Deletion

To delete your account, contact us. All your messages and data will be permanently removed.

Security Tips

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Never share your password
  • Log out on shared devices
