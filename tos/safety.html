Safety & Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Our Commitment

We want Groups to be a safe and respectful place for everyone. We expect all users to treat each other with respect.

Prohibited Behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats toward other users
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or content that promotes violence
  • Sharing explicit, pornographic, or sexually suggestive content
  • Impersonating others or creating fake accounts
  • Spamming, advertising, or unsolicited commercial messages
  • Sharing illegal content or encouraging illegal activities
  • Attempting to exploit security vulnerabilities

Reporting Abuse

If you encounter harmful behavior, please report the user by contacting us. We review all reports seriously.

Consequences

Violating these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account ban

Safety Tips

  • Never share personal information (address, phone number, etc.)
  • Be cautious when talking to strangers
  • Block and report suspicious or uncomfortable users
