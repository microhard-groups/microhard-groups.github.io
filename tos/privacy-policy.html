Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Groups collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our messaging service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, user ID, and authentication data
  • Messages: Content of messages you send and receive
  • Usage Data: Login times, features used, and interaction data
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system (automatically collected)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the messaging service
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts
  • To enable real-time communication between users
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Messages are end-to-end visible only to the participants of each chat. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Firebase servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, though absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated messages
  • Object to certain processing of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Groups is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
